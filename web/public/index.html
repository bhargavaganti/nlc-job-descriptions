Sample Apps

With the enormous number and variety of jobs available on job listing websites, narrowing down which jobs you might be qualified for is often a major challenge for potential applicants.

This sample app receives an applicant's qualifications as its input and leverages Watson Natural Language Classifier to extract the key words and phrases from your list of qualifications and matches them to the desired qualifications within job listing descriptions. Its classifier was trained using NYC Open Data's Job Category data set, an open-source data set that contains the various job titles and descriptions of jobs the City of New York has posted on its hiring website.

Click the drop down to view some examples or enter your own job description or list of qualifications and see how its classified.

Don't have a job description or resume handy? Feel free to browse Indeed to find a job description to test out!
